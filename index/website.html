WELCOME TO FORDHAM MOCK TRIAL

Rose Hill Campus: Bronx, New York

Fordham University's Mock Trial is a club that provides its members with valuable hands-on experience with court procedures and the judicial system and helps its members either practice or improve basic life skills, such as critical thinking, persuasive writing, and public speaking. Fordham Mock is a group of hardworking, passionate undergraduate students that share a common interest in either the legal field or the theatrical arena. These students can meet in an enjoyable and informative environment.

 
 

When I think about my four years at Fordham, my best memories originate from my time on the mock trial team. Without it, I would not have formed lifelong friendships or have developed the public speaking and critical thinking skills that I brought with me to law school.

Kaela Palmiter ('18)
